Trial Lesson Ukido: The Art of Open Questions

Hello, future captain of your life! Today we'll unlock one of the main secrets of communication — the ability to ask open questions.

What is this?

Closed question — one that can be answered "yes" or "no". ("Did you like the movie?").

Open question — one that makes you think and give a detailed answer. ("What exactly in this movie made you think?").

Your first task:

Tonight, ask your parents NOT "How was work?", but "What was the most interesting thing that happened to you today?".
